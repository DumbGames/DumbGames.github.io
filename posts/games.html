Deus Ex

Deus Ex is one of the best games I ever played. Despite being more than 20 years old, It is still considered a masterpiece. Do you know why my online-username is SmashTheState? It's because SMASHTHESTATE is a password to a computer terminal on the pedestal of the Staue Of Liberty in the first and one of the most iconic mission of Deus Ex. What I enjoyed most about Deus Ex was the narrative and the dark environment and the fact that somehow it resembles the post 9/11 world we live in. I think anyone with a decent attention span will enjoy Deus Ex. What? attention span? Yes this game is very deep and if you really want to enjoy it you have to have a decent attention span. If you don't, why are you even reading this go check your twitter for feeds.
